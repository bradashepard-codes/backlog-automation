# AI-Driven Backlog Automation for Enterprise Technology Delivery

## About Me

I am an enterprise technology delivery leader focused on large-scale platform modernization within complex, regulated industries. My work sits at the intersection of business strategy and engineering execution—translating organizational intent into structured, traceable delivery outcomes. I specialize in aligning product, engineering, and design teams around a shared source of truth, particularly in environments where the cost of miscommunication is high.

## Industry Focus

This assistant is purpose-built for enterprise environments undergoing digital transformation, with a particular emphasis on the property and casualty insurance sector. The persistent challenge in these environments is requirements fragmentation—business intent gets lost as it moves across teams, tools, and handoffs. This assistant addresses that gap by introducing an AI-driven semantic layer that ensures continuity between what the business wants and what engineering builds. It is especially relevant for organizations modernizing core systems such as policy, billing, and claims platforms, where precision, traceability, and governance are non-negotiable.

## Three Ways Generative AI Is Useful in This Space

- **Requirements structuring** — Converts unstructured inputs (Word documents, Excel spreadsheets, meeting notes) into well-formed epics, features, and user stories ready for engineering consumption
- **Cross-tool publishing** — Automatically propagates structured requirements to Jira, Confluence, and Figma, reducing manual re-entry and the risk of version drift across systems
- **Delivery acceleration** — Compresses the time between business alignment and sprint-ready backlog items, enabling faster iteration without sacrificing traceability or compliance requirements
